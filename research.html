Research

My research focuses on the intersection of political theory and public policy. I am particularly interested in the ways that political theory can inform public policy, and how public policy can be used to realize the ideals of political theory. I am also interested in the ways that political theory can be used to critique public policy, and how public policy can be used to critique political theory. My research is interdisciplinary, drawing on insights from political science, philosophy, economics, and sociology.
